================================================================================
THE NAME: WHY "ELIMU"
================================================================================

MEANING & ETYMOLOGY:
"Elimu" (Swahili) = Education / Knowledge / Learning
Root: Arabic "ilm" (علم) meaning knowledge
Adopted into Swahili, now deeply Kenyan

CULTURAL SIGNIFICANCE:
- Every Kenyan knows this word from childhood
- Represents hope, opportunity, social mobility
- Parents say "Elimu ni ufunguo wa maisha" (Education is the key to life)
- Carries weight and respect in Kenyan society

WHY THIS NAME WORKS:
✓ Instant recognition across all Kenyan communities
✓ No translation needed - universally understood
✓ Professional and credible
✓ Emotional connection to teachers' mission
✓ Short, memorable, and easy to pronounce
✓ Positive associations only
✓ Scales beyond Kenya (East Africa recognizes it)
